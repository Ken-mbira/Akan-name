Akan Name.

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.
These names usally differed based on gender where the name for a certain day foy a boy-child was different from that of a girl-child.
Most Ghanaians have at least one name from this system followed by their christian names, You can have this too.
